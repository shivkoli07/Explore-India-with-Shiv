# 🌏 Explore India with Shiv

Explore India with Shiv is a full-stack web-based platform that allows users to discover the diverse places, food, culture, history, and shopping experiences across India — from **Kashmir to Kanyakumari**, **Gujarat to Arunachal Pradesh**, including **Andaman & Nicobar** and **Lakshadweep**.

The platform provides an **interactive map-based experience** where users can click on cities and explore detailed information in an intuitive and visually engaging way.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🔐 User authentication (Email & Google Sign-In)
- 🌍 Interactive world map with India-focused exploration
- 📍 City-wise exploration across all Indian states & UTs
- 🏞 Best tourist places with images & location details
- 🍲 Best food spots with must-try dishes
- 🛍 Popular shopping areas & local markets
- 🥘 Local dishes with cultural significance
- 🏛 Culture & history of each city/state
- 📱 Responsive design (Desktop & Mobile friendly)

## 🗺 How It Works

1. User opens the website and sees a welcome screen.
2. User signs in using email or Google account.
3. An interactive world map is displayed.
4. User zooms into India and clicks on any city.
5. The platform displays:
   - Best places
   - Best food spots
   - Shopping locations
   - Local dishes
   - Culture & history
6. Data is fetched dynamically from the backend.
